Memoring — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Memoring is a reminder app. You type or speak what you want to remember, and the app rings at the right time. This policy explains exactly what happens to your information. The short version:

Your personal content never leaves your phone. Reminders, photos, voice text, and your onboarding answers (including religion) are stored only on your device. Memoring has no accounts, no cloud, and no analytics. The only third-party data processing is advertising (Google AdMob), described below.

1. Information stored on your device (never transmitted)

  • Reminders — text, times, repeat rules, and completion history.
  • Photos — pictures you attach to reminders and confirmation selfies. Photo checks (e.g. detecting a mosque or prayer mat) run entirely on your device; images are never uploaded.
  • Onboarding answers — your name, age range, and (optionally) religion. Religion is asked only to tailor features such as prayer-time reminders, is always optional ("Prefer not to say"), and is stored only on your device.
  • Voice input — converted to text by your device's own speech service; Memoring only receives the resulting text.

Deleting the app deletes all of this data. There is no copy anywhere else.

2. Advertising (the one exception)

Memoring shows banner ads through Google AdMob on the reminders list and insights screens. To serve ads, Google may collect device information such as advertising identifiers, IP address, and coarse device data. This is handled by Google under its own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data in AdMob apps

Your reminders, photos, and onboarding answers are not shared with Google or any advertiser. You can reset or delete your advertising ID in your phone's settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads).

3. Permissions and why we ask

  • Notifications & alarms — to ring at the exact reminder time, including full-screen alerts.
  • Camera — to attach photos to reminders and take confirmation selfies. Used only when you tap the camera.
  • Microphone — to dictate reminders. Used only while the mic button is active.
  • Start at boot — to re-schedule your alarms after the phone restarts.

4. What we never do

  • No accounts, no sign-in, no passwords.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data.
  • No reading of contacts, messages, files, or location.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs beyond the advertising described above.

5. Children

Memoring is a general-audience app and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All content stays on the device regardless of age.

6. Data deletion

Uninstalling Memoring permanently removes all app data from your device. Because we hold no copies, there is nothing further to request or delete on our side.

7. Changes

If this policy changes (for example, new ad networks or an optional cloud backup), this page will be updated with a new date, and material changes will be noted in the app.
