## App name

## Brief introduction (≤80 chars)   [79 chars]
Type or speak a reminder — unmissable alarms, prayer times, photo proof & more.

## Full introduction (≤800 chars)   [~780 chars]
Memoring turns a single sentence into a perfectly timed alarm. Type or speak
"call mom in 2 hours" and it understands — no forms, no fuss.

• Unmissable alerts: full-screen alarms that ring until you act. Choose Once,
Keep Ringing, Selfie proof (camera confirms you really did it), or Wake mode
(solve simple math to stop it).
• Life tools: document renewal warnings (ID, visa, passport), daily medicine
with photo proof, birthday reminders the evening before, and a photo Vault for
"where did I put it?"
• Prayer times for 50+ cities worldwide, fully offline.
• Voice input, Memories gallery, insights, PIN app-lock.

100% private: no account, no cloud — your reminders and photos never leave
your phone.

## New features (release notes, v1.0.0)
