## Full description (~3,300/4,000 chars)

Memoring turns one sentence into a perfectly timed alarm. Type or speak "call mom in 2 hours" — and it just works. No forms, no date pickers, no fuss.

💬 REMINDERS AS EASY AS CHATTING
Write naturally, like you're texting a friend:
• "pay rent on the 1st every month"
• "dentist appointment next Friday at 4pm"
• "take medicine daily at 9am"
Memoring understands the time, sets the alarm, and shows a clean confirmation card. Changed your mind? Just type "cancel the dentist reminder."

🎙 SPEAK INSTEAD OF TYPING
Tap the mic, say your reminder, and watch it appear in the field — review it, then send. Perfect for reminders on the go.

🔔 ALARMS YOU LITERALLY CANNOT MISS
Every reminder rings full-screen with sound and vibration — even from the lock screen. Choose how strict each alarm is:
• Once — a single gentle ring
• Keep Ringing — loops until you respond
• Selfie Proof — the alarm will not stop until you take a photo proving you did the task
• Wake Mode — solve a simple math problem to silence it (your snooze-proof morning alarm)
Dismissed alarms with a task come back until the task is actually done. No more lying to yourself.

🕌 PRAYER TIMES, FULLY OFFLINE
Accurate prayer reminders for 50+ cities worldwide, calculated on your phone with the correct regional method — no internet needed. Optional mosque-selfie confirmation keeps you accountable.

📋 LIFE ADMIN, HANDLED
• Renewals — passport, visa, ID, licence, car registration: get warned 1 month, 1 week, and 1 day before expiry
• Medicine — daily dose reminders, with optional photo proof you took it
• People — birthday reminders the evening before and on the day, every year
• Vault — snap photos of where you put things ("spare keys in the blue box") and find them by search

📸 MEMORIES & INSIGHTS
A private gallery of everything you've completed — including your proof selfies — plus friendly stats: completion rate, what you remind yourself about most, and your prayer log.

🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No account, no sign-up — open the app and start
• Your reminders, photos, and voice recordings stay on your device
• The optional religion question (for prayer times) is never transmitted
• PIN lock protects your private screens
• Only anonymous usage statistics and feedback you choose to send are collected — see our privacy policy for details

⚡ MADE FOR REAL LIFE
Works offline. Survives restarts. A 9pm preview shows tomorrow's reminders so mornings never surprise you. Announcements from us appear in-app — no spam emails, because we don't even have your email.

Memoring — never forget again.
